I am currently the **Director** of the Network Information Technology Division, Information Center, **China State Railway Group Co., Ltd.** (中国国家铁路集团有限公司信息中心网络信息技术处).

I also serve as a **Senior Engineer** at **SinoRail Information Engineering Co., Ltd.** (Beijing).

Prior to this, I served at **China Railway Container Transport Corp. (CRCT)** (中铁集装箱运输有限责任公司).

My work primarily focuses on the management and development of railway network information systems and digital infrastructure.
